ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Founded in 2020, Black Arc Esports has rapidly ascended to become one of the most respected names in competitive gaming. What began as a small group of passionate gamers has evolved into a professional organization with multiple championship-winning teams.

Our mission is to push the boundaries of esports performance while fostering a community that values excellence, integrity, and innovation. We believe in creating opportunities for talented players to shine on the global stage.
